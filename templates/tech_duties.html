Customer Service

Have you ever been to Chik-fila? They are way too nice, almost as if they knew you in a past life. That is the kind of customer service we are going for. We need our technicians to be able to talk to every client as if they are grandma. End-users often get overly frusterated with a simple problem, thinking that their issue is dire, so they need someone to listen, and respectfully help them find a solution. Remember, your rating is important for your reputation to other clients!
